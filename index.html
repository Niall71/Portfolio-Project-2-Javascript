Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock

Introduction

Welcome to the Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock game! Lets see if you can outsmart this JavaScript program, and best the computer. This is a game developed using JavaScript, it is based off of the classic rock, paper, scissors game. The match will be scored based on the results of three rounds. After your game is over if you wish to replay simply click the reset button.
